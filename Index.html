Calculus in Animal Husbandry Technology

Applying Derivatives f'(x) to Ayam Kampung Kacukan Growth Dynamics

The Bioscience Scenario

As an Animal Husbandry Technologist, you need to optimize feed efficiency. An animal doesn't grow at the same speed its whole life. By finding the derivative of the growth curve, we determine the exact Rate of Weight Gain. This tells us when the chicken is converting feed to muscle fastest (peak growth) and when growth slows down, signaling it's time for market.

Data Source Context: Realistically modeled cubic regression based on growth performance benchmarks from the Department of Veterinary Services (DVS) Malaysia and poultry research journals from Universiti Putra Malaysia (UPM) for Ayam Kampung Kacukan (0-12 weeks).

The Mathematical Models

1. Body Weight Function, W(t)

Total weight in grams at week t.

W(t) = -0.5t3 + 15t2 + 40t + 40

2. Growth Rate (Derivative), W'(t)

Grams gained per week at week t.

W'(t) = -1.5t2 + 30t + 40
